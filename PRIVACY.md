# Privacy Policy for Claude Pulse

**Last Updated: April 21, 2026**

Claude Pulse is a browser extension designed to provide real-time metrics (token counts, cache timers, and usage limits) for users of Claude.ai. Your privacy is a fundamental priority.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
Claude Pulse **does not collect, store, or transmit any user data**. 

- **Local Processing**: All tokenization, usage calculations, and countdown timers are processed entirely within your browser's local memory.
- **No Data Exfiltration**: No text from your conversations, organization IDs, or usage metrics ever leave your machine. The extension does not communicate with any external servers.
- **Access to Claude.ai**: The extension reads data from your own Claude.ai session to display it back to you. This data is never sensitive to the developer or any third party.

## 2. Permissions
The extension only runs on `https://claude.ai/*`. It uses this access solely to surface metrics that are already available in your active session. It does not require or use permissions for:
- `storage`
- `identity`
- `cookies` (beyond identifying your active Claude session locally)
- `management`

## 3. Third-Party Services
Claude Pulse does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking icons, or advertising services.

## 4. Changes to this Policy
If the extension's functionality changes in a way that affects your privacy, this policy will be updated. However, our commitment to local-only processing will remain a core principle.
